Overview

Purpose

We are excited to offer our expertise with White Water Rafting whether you are new to rafting or experienced all are welcome.

Audience

How old must you be to ride the rapids? Must be 12 yrs of age or older (no experience required.) What can you expect on the trip? Our experienced guides are ready to take you on an adventure. The guides will teach you the basics or give you a refesher course before you even get into the water. They will go over the do's and dont's for rafting. How long is the trip? That depends on you and the package you choose. We offer half-day, all day or weekend trips. Do you do groups? Yes, we love having groups come and enjoy the white water with us. Whether you are a school group, church group, or here for a business get away all are welcome. How many can fit in the raft? A total of six plus the guide will fit in the raft. So plan accordingly and make sure you have an accurate count so we know how many rafts and guides are needed for your trip. What is the price of a tour and are there discounts? The price is based off the package you choose. We offer group discounts only. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Anything with internet access
